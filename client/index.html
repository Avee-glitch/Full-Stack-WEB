About AI Harm Watch

Our Mission

AI Harm Watch is an open-source, community-driven platform dedicated to documenting and exposing the harmful effects of artificial intelligence systems.

Why We Exist

As AI becomes more integrated into society, its negative impacts are often overlooked or underreported. We provide a transparent, verifiable record of AI harms to:

  • Hold AI developers accountable
  • Inform policymakers and regulators
  • Educate the public about AI risks
  • Support affected individuals and communities

Our Principles

Transparency

Community
